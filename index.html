FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is the demo free?
Yes, the demo is completely free and carries no obligation. We won't ask for a credit card or payment information at any point during or after the session.
How long does the demo take?
Most sessions run between 30 and 45 minutes, depending on the size and complexity of your team's workflows. We leave time at the end for your questions.
Do I need technical knowledge to follow the demo?
Not at all. FlowDesk AI is designed for operations managers, team leads, and non-technical users as much as it is for developers. The demo is structured to be accessible to everyone on your team.
Can I invite my team to the demo?
Absolutely. We encourage it. Having key stakeholders present — from operations leads to technical team members — means everyone gets aligned from the start. Just let us know how many people to expect.
What happens after the demo?
After the session, we'll share a summary along with any resources relevant to your use case. There's no pressure to commit — you'll have everything you need to evaluate FlowDesk AI on your own terms.
